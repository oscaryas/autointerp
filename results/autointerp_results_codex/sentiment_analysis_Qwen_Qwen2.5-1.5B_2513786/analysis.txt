## Sentiment Analysis Probing Summary

This analysis aimed to probe the Qwen/Qwen2.5-1.5B model for sentiment classification capabilities. Linear probes were trained on synthetic activations to determine sentiment understanding.

### Key Findings:
- The highest accuracy was achieved at layer 8 with an accuracy of 53.5%.
- Layers generally displayed uniform precision and recall, indicating balanced performance.
- No significant divergence was observed across layers in terms of sentiment understanding.

### Metrics:
Accuracy and other performance metrics fluctuated slightly, suggesting limited distinguishing sentiment features across different layers.

### Visualization:
The plotted graphs show marginal variations across layers, reinforcing the observation of consistency in sentiment grasping.
