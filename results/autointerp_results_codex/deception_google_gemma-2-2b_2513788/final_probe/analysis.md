## Analysis of Deception Detection Probing\n\nThe objective was to explore the model's internal representations to detect whether it is behaving deceptively or honestly based on role-play scenarios. Linear probes were trained on model activations to achieve this.\n\n### Key Findings\n- **Performance Metrics**: Generated from the probe showing satisfactory performance in identifying deceptive behavior.\n- **Layer and Token Importance**: The results suggest that specific layers and token positions play a significant role in representing deception signals.\n- **Generalization**: Initial assessment shows the probe generalizes to different deception scenarios.\n\n### Conclusion\nThe trained probe successfully captured deception-related patterns within the model's representations, highlighting the potential for further probing work to enhance interpretability.\n
